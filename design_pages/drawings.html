My Artwork

Color Pencil Drawing


This drawing was created first as a sketch using graphite pencil. Shading and detail were added using a burnt ochre oil based color pencil from the brand Faber Castell.

Ballpoint Pen Drawing

After creating a graphite sketch of the subject, I used a blue BIC ballpoint pen to lightly shade using the crosshatching technique. Over time once I felt I had marked out details I began to crosshatch further and create more contrast in the shading.

Graphite Drawing of Carter

This drawing was done on a poster sized piece of standard drawing paper using graphite pencil. I used Faber Castell graphite pencils with an eraser, pencil eraser and kneaded eraser to sketch and shade the subject. "Carter" is a friend of mine who generously allowed me to use a photo of them as reference.

Graphite Drawing of Valentina

This was a graphite drawing done using Faber Castell graphite with an eraser and kneaded eraser. Done on an 11x14 inches bristol pad, I drew "Valentina" who is my best friend and allowed me to use a photo from her quinceanera as a reference.
